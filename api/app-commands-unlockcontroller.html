Class app\commands\UnlockController

All Classes | Properties | Methods
Inheritanceapp\commands\UnlockController » app\commands\DaemonController » yii\console\Controller
Implementsapp\models\DaemonInterface

Unlocker Daemon This daemon unlocks tickets that are trapped in a locked state and will not recover without an action.

Tickts in the bootup_lock = 1 state, that somehow fail to give notice after they successfully booted up, will be unlocked by this command.
